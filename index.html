Enhancing Navigation Bar Styling

The styling for the "nav1" class involved crucial adjustments to enhance the navigation bar's visual appeal and structure. Initially, default list styling was removed for a cleaner look ("list-style-type: none").

The background color was then set to a dark shade (#333), providing an appealing backdrop. To maintain a neat layout, overflow was hidden ("overflow: hidden"), and precise spacing control was achieved by eliminating padding and margin ("padding: 0; margin: 0").
